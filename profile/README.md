# Open Engineering Motion Pictures

Bringing engineering to life through moving images.

Open Engineering Motion Pictures is part of the Open Engineering ecosystem.

Its mission is to create, produce, and publish high-quality motion picture assets that explain, educate, inspire, and entertain through film, animation, and AI-generated video.

Rather than focusing on individual characters, stories, or artwork, Open Engineering Motion Pictures combines reusable assets from across the ecosystem into compelling visual productions.

⸻

## Why Motion Pictures?

Engineering is dynamic.

Many engineering concepts are difficult to communicate using static documentation alone.

Motion pictures can demonstrate:

* systems interacting
* software evolving over time
* engineering workflows
* architectural transformations
* cloud infrastructure
* animations
* simulations
* tutorials
* product demonstrations
* educational series
* documentaries
* conference presentations

By combining reusable ecosystem assets, a single production can communicate ideas that would otherwise require pages of documentation.

⸻

## Mission

Our mission is to build an open production studio for engineering.

We develop reusable assets, workflows, tooling, and best practices for creating engineering videos.

This includes:

* AI-generated video
* live-action recordings
* animation
* motion graphics
* visual effects
* virtual production
* cinematography
* editing
* sound design
* narration
* subtitles
* publishing

Everything is treated as reusable engineering assets rather than one-off productions.

⸻

## Building Blocks

## Relationship to Other Organizations

Open Engineering Motion Pictures does not own:

* characters
* stories
* maps
* branding
* educational content

Instead, it assembles those reusable components into complete visual productions.

This separation of concerns keeps every asset reusable across many different projects.

⸻

We envision a future where engineering knowledge can be experienced as naturally as watching a film.
